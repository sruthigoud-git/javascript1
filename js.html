Principal Suspects in the Heists

The Professor (Sergio Marquina)

Alias: "The Professor"

A highly intelligent and elusive mastermind behind major heists, including the Royal Mint and Bank of Spain. Known for his strategic brilliance and ability to stay ahead of law enforcement.

Berlin (Andrés de Fonollosa)

Alias: "Berlin"

A highly charismatic and manipulative criminal, Berlin is known for his role as the second-in-command during the Royal Mint heist. With a history of violent crimes he is considered both a strategic mastermind and ruthless individual.

Nairobi (Ágata Jiménez)

Alias: "Nairobi"

A skilled and ruthless criminal, Nairobi is known for her leadership role in the Royal Mint and Bank of Spain heists. Formerly a counterfeit expert, she is feared for her quick thinking and ability to handle high-pressure situations.
